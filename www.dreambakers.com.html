About Us

Dream Bakers is a dedicated home-based bakery located in B-17 Islamabad. We specialize in beautifully crafted customized cakes, cupcakes, brownies, and donuts. We proudly take orders from all over Islamabad and Taxila, ensuring that every product is freshly prepared with quality ingredients. Customers are requested to place their orders at least one day in advance, and additional charges may apply based on the complexity of the design. To explore our creations, feel free to visit our Instagram page: @drea_mbakers20.
